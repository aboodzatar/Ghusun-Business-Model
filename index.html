The Problem

Farmers Are Left Behind

Enterprise smart-farming systems cost thousands of dollars and require dedicated technical staff — leaving small Jordanian farmers unable to afford automation.

Our Answer

Two Paths. Zero Friction.

We meet the farmer exactly where they are — no infrastructure destruction required, installation in hours.

Option A — Retrofit Mode

We connect smart relay switches to the farmer's existing pumps and fans. If they have a manual button for ventilation, a relay sits inline and GHUSUN "presses" it digitally — zero infrastructure destruction.

2–4 hour installation
No hardware replacement
Lowest barrier to entry
Ideal for existing greenhouses

Option B — Full Turnkey Kit

GHUSUN provides the complete hardware stack — ESP32-S3 controller, sensor array, irrigation pump, ventilation fan, and LED grow lights — all pre-wired, tested, and installed by our team.

Pre-wired and tested
On-site installation
Weatherproof enclosure
Higher revenue per customer

How We Make Money

Three Revenue Streams, One Product

01
Hardware Sales
One-time revenue from the GHUSUN controller, sensor kits, and optional full actuator kit. Healthy margin built above raw component cost. Retrofit kits are lower cost but higher volume.
02
SaaS Subscription
Monthly recurring revenue from software access, AI decision quotas, dashboard features, and support tiers. Per-user backend costs decrease as the user base grows — revenue scales linearly.
03
AI Overage & Add-ons
Pay-per-use revenue when farmers exceed their AI quota. Additional plant profiles, advanced analytics exports, and custom reporting for cooperatives or government partners.
04
Data & Partnerships — Future
As GHUSUN accumulates anonymized farm data across Jordan, this dataset becomes valuable to seed companies, the Ministry of Agriculture, and NGOs like FAO — a realistic long-term B2G channel.

Technical Moat

From Cloud LLM to Local Intelligence

Our AI roadmap systematically eliminates ongoing API costs while building a Jordanian agricultural model no enterprise system can replicate.

Phase 1 — Current

Cloud AI API

Powered by OpenAI API (GPT-4o-mini). Agentic reasoning with confidence scores and severity levels. Cost-effective at small scale — the right approach for early deployment.

Live & Running
Phase 2 — 1–2 Years

Fine-Tuned Model

LLM-generated decisions become labeled training data. A lightweight model (LSTM or TinyLLaMA) trained on GHUSUN-specific Jordanian agricultural data runs on our own servers.

10–50× cost reduction
Phase 3 — 3+ Years

Local On-Device AI

LLaMA-based model deployed locally on each farm. No internet required. Full data privacy. Zero ongoing API costs. A Jordan-specific AI that no enterprise system currently offers.

Full data sovereignty
